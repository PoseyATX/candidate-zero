## Balance breadth (supports the "brutal but winnable" covenant)

`npm run harness:matrix` — structured sample, N=30/cell, 93 cells across 24
personas × 4 districts × 7 regions:

- **No soft-locks, no free wins.** Every persona reaches the ballot ~87–100%,
  every persona can reach the general, every persona can win. Overall-win band
  **3.3%–33.3%** (open/east labor). The `+$1,500` `smallbiz` persona sits at the
  **low** end of the win axis (3.3%) — the extra cash buys ballot access, not a
  degenerate win.
- **Wrong-party stress** (labor, unique east personas): 22/24 personas still
  win; mean overall win **9.2%** — the hardest identity is punishing, not
  impossible.

## What v0.1 does **not** claim

- Not feature-complete: Phase 8 (Unity presentation → iOS) is the live roadmap.
- Not fully audited for a11y beyond automated axe-core — keyboard focus-order
  flow and screen-reader copy review remain manual.
- Balance is sampled, not exhaustively solved across the full 12k setup grid
  (issues are flavor for win math; see `docs/BALANCE-NOTES.md`).
